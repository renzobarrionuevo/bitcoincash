On August 1, 2017 a group of Bitcoin miners, developers, investors and users decided to update the protocol as specified. As the community did not reach a unanimous agreement, the miners parted safely, creating a new version of Bitcoin called "Bitcoin Cash".
The existing ledger at the time of the division is retained, so that users who owned Bitcoin before block 478,558 also went on to own Bitcoin Cash in equal amount.
Bitcoin Cash brings sound money to the world. Merchants and users are empowered with low fees and reliable confirmations. The future shines brightly with unrestricted growth, global adoption, permissionless innovation, and decentralized development.
