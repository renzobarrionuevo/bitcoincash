What is Bitcoin Cash Exchange?

Cryptocurrency exchanges are online platforms that facilitate trading between different cryptocurrencies or between cryptocurrencies and conventional fiat money. In a Bitcoin Cash Exchange you can buy Bitcoin Cash (BCH) using the payment methods and currencies allowed by the platform, or conversely sell your Bitcoin Cash (BCH) in exchange for other currencies or fiat money.


Crypto exchanges differentiate themselves between Market Makers and Matching Platforms. The former is the most common type, being analogous to the physical currency exchange where you buy or sell currencies at a publicly quoted price. The later connects buyers and sellers of the same cryptocurrencies. This is achieved by using algorithms to match between sell orders and buy orders for a given cryptocurrency.
